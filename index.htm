HotGato Speed Reader


About this service

  • 100% offline, zero dependencies, no servers, nothing. IT approved.
  • Font selector includes Dyslexia and ADHD presets.
  • ADHD font highlights the first two letters of every word.
  • Speed automatically slows for all numbers and special characters.
  • Your settings are remembered in your browser for convenience.
  • You can download this for offline use via Github

Note from the author

When I was a research assistant in grad school I leaned on Spreeder like my life depended on it. I even submitted a suggestion for Kindle for Android to build the feature, and half a year later Amazon released WordRunner. My ADHD hits hardest when reading long documents, and I've oft griped about authors hiding details where I would never find them - in paragraphs and long lists. I built this tool so I can benefit from my preferred speed reading technique for confidential materials. This app is entirely offline, and supports a handful of features competitors like Spreeder and WordRunner lack.

- Robert Allen (Github) July 2023
